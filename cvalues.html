EVSU Mission, Vision, Core Values


Vision

A Leading State University in Technological and Professional Education.

Mission

Develop a Strong Technologically and Professionally Competent Productive Human Resource Imbued with Positive Values Needed to Propel Sustainable Development.

Core Values

Excellence - A personal and collective adherence to high standards and distinctive achievement.

Value-laden - Guided with the highest practice of ethics, principles and moral standards.

Service-driven - With the commitment and motivation to exist with relevance and significance in providing help and assistance to the deserving.

Unity in Diversity - In agreement with one another respecting each other’s worth and variety of beliefs and talents towards a synergistic goal.
